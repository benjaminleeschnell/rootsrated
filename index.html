About RootsRated

Founded in 2012, RootsRated’s mission is to connect people to the best outdoor experiences by enabling brands and retailers to communicate accessible experiences to their customers. We do this through the editorial production of high quality local content that is then distributed through our owned and operated media products and licensed to outdoor brands and retailers for distribution to their customers. We have a successful team made up of skilled employees and industry experts, and our investors include a group of the most influential CEOs in the outdoor industry. We're tackling complex problems with deep domain expertise and a holistic view of platform creation in one of the most exciting and rewarding industries. It doesn't get better than the outdoor industry, and we live and breathe the outdoor lifestyle.
