Your Impact

RootsRated is looking for Software Engineers to improve the fastest growing online media company in the outdoor industry. RootsRated’s applications are written using the Ruby on Rails web framework. Candidates will write Ruby and incorporate dynamic behavior with JavaScript as appropriate. As with any web application, they’ll need to assist with building HTML/CSS, integrating with backend databases & external services, and optimizing for mobile. Our Software Engineer will work with Technical and Executive Leadership to continue development of our website, mobile app, communications, customer facing features, and internal portals.

As a developer on a small team, you will take complete ownership of the features you work on. The work you do will be interacted with by tens of thousands of people and will improve the way they see and experience our company and services. The RootsRated team promotes a collaborative environment using industry best practices such as daily standup meetings, weekly company meetings, code reviews, and pair programming. Peer critiques and constructive criticism allows the tech team to grow and learn at a fast pace.

About RootsRated

Founded in 2012, RootsRated’s mission is to connect people to the best outdoor experiences, by enabling brands and retailers to communicate accessible experiences to their customers. We do this through the editorial production of high quality local content that is then distributed through our owned and operated media products and licensed to outdoor brands and retailers for distribution to their customers.

We have a successful team made up of skilled employees and industry experts, and our investors include a group of the most influential CEOs in the outdoor industry. We're tackling complex problems with deep domain expertise and a holistic view of platform creation in one of the most exciting and rewarding industries. It doesn't get better than the outdoor industry, and we live and breathe the outdoor lifestyle
