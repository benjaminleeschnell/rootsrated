About RootsRated

RootsRated was founded in the Summer of 2012. While on a cross-country road trip, Fynn Glover was inspired to create a tool that would simplify the way people discover and access the most memorable outdoor recreation. As he jumped from state to state, he found himself wishing he could ask locals for their take on the best outdoor experiences in their area. Recognizing this as broad need for people across the US, Fynn started developing a concept to help people share their insights. Shortly after, Fynn partnered with Dawson Wheeler and Mark McKnight, who’d grown to recognize this same need from their vantage points as outdoor retailers.
